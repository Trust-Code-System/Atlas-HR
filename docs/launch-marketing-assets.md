## Demo Video Script

Target length: 90 seconds.

1. 0-10s: The pain: HR teams lose time switching between search, old templates, and one-off advice.
2. 10-30s: Atlas HR overview: knowledge, tools, templates, community, Copilot.
3. 30-50s: Generate an HR document from a real scenario.
4. 50-70s: Ask Copilot a country-aware HR question and save the result.
5. 70-85s: Show templates, Knowledge Hub, and community.
6. 85-90s: CTA: start free or request beta access.
