# Atlas HR Case Study Candidates

Use this tracker at the end of Phase E to identify three customers per tier for sales proof.

## Selection Criteria

- Team: active workspace usage, at least 20 employees, recurring use of leave, compliance, lifecycle, or activity history.
- Business: use of Business-only needs such as custom reports, advanced compliance flags, helpdesk, surveys, custom workflows, or branding.
- Enterprise pilot: 250+ employees, named executive sponsor, measurable implementation milestone, and permission to quote after pilot.

## Interview Format

1. Company context: size, country, HR team structure, previous tools.
2. Before Atlas HR: operational pain, time lost, compliance risk, employee experience gaps.
3. Atlas HR rollout: features used, implementation timeline, blockers.
4. Outcome: time saved, faster approvals, compliance improvement, self-service adoption, reporting quality.
5. Quote approval: named quote, anonymous quote, or logo-only reference.
