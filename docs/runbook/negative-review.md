# Runbook: Negative Public Review

## Symptoms

- Critical Product Hunt, LinkedIn, Reddit, or community comment.
- Public complaint about pricing, AI accuracy, missing local coverage, or a bug.
- User says Atlas HR is misleading, broken, or not useful.

## Response Principles

- Reply calmly and specifically.
- Thank them for the concrete feedback.
- Do not argue, minimize, or ask for upvotes.
- If they found a real issue, say what you will do next.
- If you cannot fix it, explain why honestly.

## Response Template

Thanks for calling this out. You are right that [specific issue] matters for HR teams. We are [current action or limitation]. I am going to [next step], and I will update this thread when it is fixed or when we have a clearer answer.

## Escalation

- Security or privacy claim: move to private channel and investigate immediately.
- Legal/compliance claim: do not improvise legal claims. Ask for details and review.
- Product bug: create an issue, patch if urgent, and reply when shipped.
- Pricing complaint: capture it, look for patterns, do not change pricing reactively.

## Follow-Up

- Log the issue in beta feedback or support.
- Add a help article if the critique reveals confusion.
- Include repeated themes in launch-week review.
