# Enterprise Pilot Program

Atlas HR's first Enterprise pilots should be structured, measurable, and narrow enough to convert into reference customers.

## Offer

- 90-day pilot for companies with 250+ employees or serious compliance complexity.
- 50% off first-year Enterprise pricing if they convert after the pilot.
- Right to cancel at pilot end if agreed success metrics are not met.

## Timeline

- Month 1: implementation, employee import, roles, workflow setup, admin training.
- Month 2: active use, weekly check-ins, adoption review, workflow tuning.
- Month 3: evaluation, ROI summary, security/legal review, order form and conversion decision.

## Success metrics

- HR admins use Atlas HR weekly for employee records, compliance, approvals, or lifecycle workflows.
- At least one employee-facing workflow runs through the portal.
- Compliance center identifies and closes real document gaps.
- Managers complete at least one approval or lifecycle task.
- Sponsor agrees Atlas HR has a clear system-of-record use case.

## Implementation owner checklist

- Create `/admin/implementations` project and assign default tasks.
- Create workspace and `/workspace/getting-started` checklist.
- Agree import template owner and deadline.
- Confirm pilot sponsor, HR admin owner, and security/legal contact.
- Schedule weekly check-ins before kickoff.
