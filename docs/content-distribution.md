# Content Distribution Plan

## Organic Channels

- Google Search: submit sitemap, monitor Search Console, improve pages with high impressions and low CTR.
- LinkedIn: weekly HR operations posts based on Knowledge Hub content and launch lessons.
- Reddit and HR communities: contribute useful answers first; do not drop promotional links without context.
- HR Slack/Discord communities: participate before sharing Atlas HR content.
- Hacker News: reserve for a focused Show HN or major milestone.

## Nigerian Launch Focus

- LinkedIn posts for Nigerian HR operators and founders.
- HR Connect Nigeria, NigeriaHR, Lagos HR Network, and local HR groups.
- Lagos and Abuja HR events.
- Local press angles for African SaaS and practical AI for HR teams.

## Partnerships

- Guest posts with HR newsletters and HR operators.
- Cross-promotion with non-competing tools, podcasts, and communities.
- Apply for HR certification partnerships once learning content is deep enough.

## Paid Channels After Launch

- Google Ads for long-tail HR document and policy queries.
- LinkedIn Ads for employer brand and HR manager segments.
- Sponsorships in HR newsletters once activation and conversion are proven.

## Four-Week Refinement Loop

After four weeks of production data:

1. Improve the top 10 pages by traffic for conversion.
2. Rewrite titles/descriptions for high-impression, low-CTR queries.
3. Fix pages with high exit rates.
4. Add internal links to orphan or low-discovery pages.
5. Use support tickets and search queries to choose the next content drop.
