# Master Services Agreement Template

Status: internal draft for lawyer review.

## Parties

This Master Services Agreement is between Atlas HR and the customer named in the applicable Order Form.

## Services

Atlas HR provides HR knowledge, document generation, workforce records, employee self-service, workflow, reporting, and compliance-support software.

## Customer responsibilities

The customer is responsible for lawful HR decisions, accuracy of uploaded data, employee notices, payroll execution, and final review of AI-assisted outputs.

## Data protection

The Data Processing Addendum governs personal data processing where applicable.

## Security

Atlas HR will maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards appropriate to HR data.

## Fees

Fees, billing cadence, pilot terms, and renewal dates are defined in the Order Form.

## Termination

Either party may terminate according to the Order Form and any uncured material breach provisions approved by counsel.

## Disclaimers

Atlas HR does not provide legal advice, payroll processing, benefits brokerage, or background checks.
