# Contract Template Suite

These are product-side working drafts for Business and Enterprise sales. They are not legal advice and must be reviewed by counsel before customer use.

## Templates

- `msa-template.md`: master services terms.
- `order-form-template.md`: customer-specific commercial terms.
- `baa-template.md`: healthcare-specific placeholder if a customer may involve PHI.
- Existing DPA material from earlier phases should be lawyer-reviewed and attached where required.

Budget legal review at $3K-$5K before relying on these in paid Enterprise deals.
