## Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Atlas HR collects, uses, shares, retains, and protects personal information. It applies to visitors, account holders, community users, organisation admins, invited teammates, and employees whose information is processed in Mini-HRIS.

Atlas HR is designed for use across the United States, European Union, United Kingdom, Nigeria, and other jurisdictions. This draft references GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and Nigeria's data protection framework, but it requires legal review before launch.

## Information We Collect

We collect account data such as name, email, password authentication state, role, organisation membership, billing identifiers, and notification preferences. We collect profile data such as country, industry, company size, theme preferences, onboarding answers, and optional avatar.

We collect user content, including generated documents, templates completed by users, Copilot conversations, community threads, replies, saved items, comments, organisation records, employee records, leave records, performance information, and files uploaded to the service.

We collect usage and technical data, including device identifiers, hashed security signals, approximate location when available, IP-derived information, browser metadata, log data, product usage, feature events, and cookies subject to the Cookie Policy.

## How We Use Information

We use personal information to provide the service, authenticate users, maintain accounts, generate documents, operate Copilot, support community features, process subscriptions, send transactional email, prevent abuse, secure accounts, respond to support requests, improve product quality, and comply with law.

For GDPR purposes, our lawful bases may include contract performance, legitimate interests, consent, legal obligation, and, where applicable, the customer's instructions as controller. Consent is used for optional marketing or analytics where required.

## When We Share Information

We share information with subprocessors that help operate Atlas HR. These may include managed providers for database, authentication, hosting, payments, tax, transactional email, AI processing, consented analytics, error monitoring, and operational support. Each provider processes data for a defined operational purpose, and the current provider register is available to customers on request.

We may share information when required by law, to enforce rights, to protect security, during a corporate transaction, or with organisation admins who manage team workspaces.

## International Data Transfers

Atlas HR may process and store information outside your country. For EU, UK, and similar users, transfers may rely on Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions, supplementary safeguards, or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

## Retention

Account data is kept until account deletion unless law requires longer retention. Security device records are retained for a limited period. Free-tier generated documents may be deleted after 30 days. Paid-tier generated documents are retained until deletion. Email logs, webhook records, usage records, and notifications are retained according to the retention schedule implemented by Atlas HR.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or port personal information. You may withdraw consent where processing depends on consent. EU users may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California users may request to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale or sharing, limit sensitive personal information, and receive non-discriminatory treatment. Nigerian users may contact the NDPC or exercise rights under applicable Nigerian data protection law.

## Exercising Rights

You can manage account data in settings, export data where available, or contact privacy@atlashr.xyz. We may verify your identity before acting on a request. Organisation employee data requests may need to be directed to the organisation that controls the data.

## Children

Atlas HR is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

## Security

We use technical and organisational measures including TLS, encryption at rest where provided by infrastructure, access controls, role-based permissions, audit logging where available, service-role separation, webhook verification, and incident response procedures. No system is perfectly secure.

## Data Breach Response

If a breach requiring notification occurs, Atlas HR will assess, contain, document, and notify regulators or affected users within legally required timelines, including 72-hour notification where GDPR or Nigerian law applies.

## Nigerian Users

Atlas HR operates from Nigeria. Nigerian users may contact privacy@atlashr.xyz with privacy requests and may escalate complaints to the Nigeria Data Protection Commission where applicable.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be announced through the service or email where legally required. Version history is available at [/legal/changes](/legal/changes).

## Contact

Contact privacy@atlashr.xyz for privacy questions, data subject requests, or DPO-related enquiries.
