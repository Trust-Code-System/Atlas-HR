## Changing your password (when logged in)

1. Go to **Settings → Security**.
2. Click **Change password**.
3. Enter your **current password**.
4. Enter and confirm your **new password** (minimum 8 characters).
5. Click **Save**.

You'll receive a confirmation email. All other active sessions will be signed out — you'll need to sign in again on other devices.

## Resetting a forgotten password

If you can't log in because you've forgotten your password:

1. Go to [atlashr.app/forgot-password](/forgot-password).
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your inbox for the reset email (also check spam).
4. Click the link and enter a new password.
5. The link expires after **1 hour** — request a new one if it's expired.

## Password requirements

Atlas HR passwords must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least one letter and one number

Strong passwords use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. We recommend using a password manager.

## Signed in with Google?

If you created your account with **Continue with Google**, you don't have an Atlas HR password. Your Google account's password is used instead. To change it, update your Google account password at [myaccount.google.com](https://myaccount.google.com).

To add an Atlas HR password (and keep using Google as an option), go to **Settings → Security → Set password**. This lets you sign in either way.

## Two-factor authentication

Atlas HR does not currently offer two-factor authentication (2FA) as a standalone feature. If you require 2FA, sign in via Google and enable 2FA on your Google account.
