## Saving a document

After generating a document, click **Save to library** in the document toolbar. You can optionally give the document a custom title (e.g. "Offer letter — Jordan Ahmed — May 2026") before saving.

Documents are saved to your personal library and persist indefinitely — there is no expiry.

## Accessing your library

Click **Library** in the main sidebar to see all your saved documents. Documents are listed in reverse chronological order by default.

## Searching and filtering

Use the search bar at the top of the Library to find documents by title or content. You can also filter by:

- **Tool type** (e.g. only offer letters, only performance reviews)
- **Date range**

## Renaming a document

Click the **···** menu on any saved document and select **Rename**. Changes are saved immediately.

## Deleting a document

Click the **···** menu and select **Delete**. Deleted documents cannot be recovered. If you may need the document later, download it first.

## Shared library (Team plan)

On the Team plan, workspace admins can share specific documents to a **shared library** visible to all workspace members. To share a document:

1. Open the document in your library.
2. Click **Share with workspace**.

Shared documents are read-only for members — only the owner or an admin can edit or delete them.

## Library storage limits

There are no storage limits on the number of documents you can save. The library is not capped on free or paid plans.
