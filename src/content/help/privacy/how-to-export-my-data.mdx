You own your data. Atlas HR lets you export a complete copy of everything associated with your account. Here's how to do it.

## What's included in the export

Your data export is a JSON file that includes:

- **Profile information** — your name, email, company, role, and account settings
- **Generated documents** — all documents you've created using Atlas HR tools, with the inputs used to generate them
- **Saved templates** — any templates you've saved and customised
- **Document library** — if you're on Pro or above, all documents stored in your library
- **Copilot conversation history** — all conversations from the date you started using Copilot
- **Workspace data** — if you're the workspace admin, this includes organisation settings (not employee records — those are exported separately)

<Callout type="info">
Employee records are **not** included in a personal account data export. If you're an HR Admin who needs a copy of employee data (for GDPR subject access requests, for example), use the separate export function in People → export, or contact us for assistance.
</Callout>

## How to export your data

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Data**.
2. Click **Export my data**.
3. Confirm the export by clicking **Request export**.
4. You'll receive an email to your account email with a download link when the export is ready.
</Steps>

The export typically takes 5–15 minutes to prepare. Once ready, the download link is valid for **48 hours**.

## About the JSON format

Your export arrives as a `.json` file. This is a structured text format readable by:

- Any text editor (open it in Notepad, VS Code, etc.)
- JSON viewers (paste it into [jsonviewer.stack.hu](https://jsonviewer.stack.hu) for a readable tree view)
- Excel or Google Sheets (import → JSON)
- Developer tools for building further processing

If you're not technically inclined and just want to view your documents, they're also included as readable text within the JSON. Each document has a `content` field with the full text.

<Callout type="tip">
If you need your documents in DOCX or PDF format rather than JSON, re-download them individually from the document library before requesting an account deletion or cancelling your subscription.
</Callout>

## Frequency of exports

You can export your data as many times as you like. There's no limit on export frequency.

## GDPR and data rights

If you're in the EU or UK, you have a right to access your personal data under GDPR / UK GDPR. The export feature fulfils this right. If you need the data in a specific format for legal purposes, or need a human-readable version rather than JSON, contact us and we'll assist.
