Security is foundational to what we do. You're trusting us with sensitive HR data — employee records, documents, contracts. Here's how we protect it.

## Our infrastructure

Atlas HR runs on **Supabase**, which uses **PostgreSQL** as its database. Here's what that means for your data:

**Row-level security (RLS):** Every database query is filtered by user identity at the database level. This means even if there were a bug in the application code, it's architecturally impossible for one user to access another user's data. Your data is isolated from other organisations' data at the database row level.

**Encryption at rest:** All data stored in Atlas HR's database is encrypted at rest using AES-256 — industry standard encryption. This includes employee records, documents, Copilot conversations, and all other stored data.

**Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between your browser and Atlas HR's servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher. We do not support unencrypted HTTP connections.

<Callout type="info">
If you see "https://" and a padlock icon in your browser's address bar when using Atlas HR, your connection is encrypted. We enforce HTTPS-only access — there's no fallback to HTTP.
</Callout>

## Authentication and access

- **Password hashing:** We never store your password in plain text. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage.
- **Session tokens:** Authentication tokens expire and are rotated automatically.
- **Google Sign-In:** If you use Google authentication, your password is never held by Atlas HR — authentication is managed entirely by Google's systems.
- **Workspace access controls:** Role-based access (Admin vs Member) is enforced at both the application and database levels.

## AI and your data

When you use Atlas Copilot or any AI document generator:

- The content of your messages and documents is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing.
- Anthropic processes this data under their API terms, which include data protection commitments.
- Your data is **not used to train AI models** under the API tier we use.
- Copilot conversations are stored in your account and only visible to you.

<Callout type="warning">
Do not share sensitive personal data (such as national insurance numbers, full bank details, or medical records) with Copilot. Use it for HR guidance and document drafting, not as a database for personal records.
</Callout>

## Data hosting and residency

By default, Atlas HR stores data on servers located in the **EU (Ireland)**. This means your data is subject to EU data protection standards by default.

For Business plan customers who require data to remain in a specific geography, contact us to discuss dedicated hosting options.

## SOC 2 Type II

We are actively working toward SOC 2 Type II certification. This is an independent security audit that verifies our controls around security, availability, and confidentiality. We expect to complete the certification process in the coming months.

In the meantime, we're happy to answer specific security questions from enterprise customers or share our security documentation on request.

## Incident response

If we ever become aware of a security incident affecting your data:

- We will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware (as required by GDPR)
- We will publish a full incident report once the investigation is complete
- We will take immediate steps to contain and remediate the issue

## Reporting a security issue

If you discover a potential security vulnerability in Atlas HR, please contact us at security@atlashr.app. We take all reports seriously and respond within one business day. We don't have a formal bug bounty programme yet, but we acknowledge and credit responsible disclosures.
