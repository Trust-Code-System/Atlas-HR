Deleting your Atlas HR account is permanent. We want to make sure you have the full picture before you proceed.

## Before you delete

**Export your data first.** Once your account is deleted, we cannot recover your documents, templates, or conversation history. Download an export from Settings → Data → Export my data before proceeding.

**Cancel your subscription first.** If you're on a paid plan, cancel your subscription first (Settings → Billing → Manage → Cancel) to avoid being charged after deletion.

**Workspace admins:** If you're the only admin of a workspace, deleting your account will also archive the workspace and remove access for all workspace members. Transfer ownership to another admin first if needed.

## How to delete your account

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Data**.
2. Scroll to the "Delete account" section.
3. Click **Delete my account**.
4. Read the confirmation screen carefully — it lists exactly what will be deleted.
5. Type **DELETE** in the confirmation field (uppercase, exactly).
6. Click **Permanently delete account**.
</Steps>

Your account is immediately deactivated. You're logged out and cannot log back in.

<Callout type="warning">
This action cannot be undone. Once you type DELETE and confirm, there is no grace period or recovery option. Please export your data first.
</Callout>

## What happens to your data

**Immediately on deletion:**
- Your account and profile are deactivated
- You can no longer log in
- Your name and email are removed from active records
- Workspace membership is terminated

**Within 30 days:**
- Your generated documents and conversation history are permanently deleted from our servers
- Your data is removed from backups on a rolling basis

**What is retained longer:**
- Billing and payment records are retained for as long as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records)
- Anonymised, aggregated usage data (not linked to you personally) may be retained for product analytics

<Callout type="info">
The 30-day deletion window exists so we can fulfil any legal obligations (for example, if there's an active support dispute or a chargeback in progress). After 30 days, your data is gone permanently.
</Callout>

## What about employee records?

If you were an HR Admin and your workspace contained employee records, those records are not automatically deleted when your account is deleted. They remain in the workspace under the remaining admin's control. This is intentional — employee data belongs to the organisation, not to the individual HR admin's personal account.

If you need employee data to be deleted as part of a GDPR request, contact us separately.

## I deleted my account by mistake

If you deleted your account and it has been less than 30 days, contact us immediately. Depending on the deletion stage, we may be able to restore it. After 30 days, restoration is not possible.
