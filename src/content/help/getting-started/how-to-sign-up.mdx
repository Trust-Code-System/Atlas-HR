Getting started with Atlas HR takes less than five minutes. Here's exactly what to expect.

## What you need before you begin

You don't need much. Have these ready:

- A valid email address (this becomes your login)
- Your full name
- Your company or organisation name (you can change this later)

You don't need a credit card to sign up. All new accounts start on the free plan, and you'll get a 14-day Pro trial automatically — no payment details required.

## Step 1: Go to the sign-up page

Head to [atlashr.app/sign-up](https://atlashr.app/sign-up). You can sign up with:

- **Email and password** — enter your email, create a password (at least 8 characters), and continue.
- **Google Sign-In** — click "Continue with Google" and authorise Atlas HR in one click.

<Callout type="tip">
Using Google Sign-In is the fastest option. It skips email verification entirely and logs you straight in.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Verify your email

If you signed up with email and password, check your inbox for a verification email from **hello@atlashr.app**. Click the "Confirm your email" button inside. The link is valid for 24 hours.

If the email doesn't arrive within two minutes:

1. Check your spam or junk folder.
2. Add **hello@atlashr.app** to your contacts and try again.
3. On the sign-in page, click "Resend verification email."

<Callout type="warning">
You won't be able to access your account until your email is verified. If you're stuck, our support team can manually verify accounts — just reach out.
</Callout>

## Step 3: You're in

Once verified, you'll land on the onboarding wizard. It takes about two minutes and helps Atlas HR personalise your experience. You can skip any step and come back later from your profile settings.

Your 14-day Pro trial starts the moment your account is confirmed — so you have full access to all Pro features right away.

## Frequently asked questions about signing up

**Can I use a work email from a custom domain?**
Yes, any valid email address works. There's no restriction on domain.

**Can I have more than one account?**
Each email address maps to one account. If you need a team workspace, use the Members feature to invite colleagues — no need for separate accounts.

**I signed up but can't log in. What do I do?**
First check that your email is verified. If it is, try resetting your password via "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page. Still stuck? Contact us at the link below.
