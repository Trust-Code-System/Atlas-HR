Atlas HR has four plans. Most people start on Free and upgrade when they hit a limit that matters to their workflow. Here's how to think through which plan is right for you.

## Free — for individuals exploring Atlas HR

The Free plan is genuinely useful. You get:

- Up to **5 document generations per month** (job descriptions, offer letters, policies, etc.)
- **20 Atlas Copilot messages per day**
- Access to the full Knowledge Hub
- One user (yourself)

Free is best if you're an HR professional experimenting with AI-assisted document drafting, or a founder who occasionally needs a policy or letter.

**Upgrade when:** you generate more than 5 documents a month, or you want to save and organise your documents.

## Pro — for active HR professionals

Pro removes the most frustrating limits:

- **Unlimited document generations**
- **Unlimited Atlas Copilot messages**
- Document library with search and folders
- Template customisation and saving
- CSV export from the document library

Pro is best for HR managers, HR consultants, or ops people who create HR documents regularly and want to stop running out of generations mid-month.

<Callout type="tip">
Pro is billed monthly or annually. Annual billing saves you roughly two months compared to monthly. Switch to annual any time from Settings → Billing.
</Callout>

**Upgrade when:** you need team members or employee records — that's the Team plan.

## Team — for HR teams managing employees

Team includes everything in Pro, plus:

- **Up to 10 admin/member seats**
- **Mini-HRIS** — employee profiles, leave management, documents, and compliance tracking
- **Employee portal** — employees get their own login to submit leave requests and view documents
- Approval workflows
- Onboarding templates
- Headcount and leave reports

Team is best for small HR teams, people managers at growing companies, or solo HR managers at companies with 10–100 employees.

**Upgrade when:** you need more than 10 seats, or you need multiple workspace environments.

## Business — for HR departments and consultants with multiple clients

Business includes everything in Team, plus:

- **Unlimited seats**
- **Multiple workspaces** — manage clients or subsidiaries separately
- Priority support with a dedicated response channel
- Custom branding on generated documents
- API access for integrations

Business is best for HR consultancies managing multiple client workspaces, or HR departments at companies with 100+ employees.

## Comparing plans at a glance

| Feature | Free | Pro | Team | Business |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Document generations | 5/month | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Copilot messages | 20/day | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Seats | 1 | 1 | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| Mini-HRIS | — | — | Yes | Yes |
| Employee portal | — | — | Yes | Yes |
| Multiple workspaces | — | — | — | Yes |

<Callout type="info">
All new accounts get a free 14-day Pro trial. No card required. After 14 days, you move to Free unless you subscribe. If you're on the trial, try the document library and Copilot — those are the features Pro users value most.
</Callout>
