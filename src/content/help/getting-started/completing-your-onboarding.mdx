When you first log in to Atlas HR, you'll see a short onboarding wizard. It's designed to take about two minutes, and it helps Atlas HR set up your workspace and personalise the tools you see. Here's what each step does.

## Step 1: Tell us about yourself

The first step asks for:

- **Your full name** — used on documents and your profile
- **Your role** — for example "HR Manager," "Founder," or "Operations Lead." This helps us surface relevant tools and templates
- **Your company size** — so we know whether to suggest Mini-HRIS features or simpler single-user tools

None of these are locked in. You can update your name, role, and company details any time from **Settings → Account**.

## Step 2: Set up your workspace

The second step creates your organisation workspace. You'll enter:

- **Organisation name** — appears on generated documents and the employee portal
- **Your industry** — helps Atlas Copilot give more relevant HR guidance
- **Primary country** — sets default labour law context for generated content

<Callout type="info">
If you're a freelance HR consultant managing multiple clients, just use your own name or consultancy name as the organisation name. You can create separate workspaces for each client once you're on the Business plan.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Choose your goals

The final step lets you tell us what you're here to do. Options include things like:

- Generate HR documents faster
- Learn HR best practices
- Manage employee records
- Stay compliant with labour law

Selecting your goals customises your dashboard — you'll see more relevant tool shortcuts and knowledge articles on your home screen.

## Skipping and finishing later

Every step has a "Skip for now" link at the bottom. If you click it, your account is created with defaults and you'll land on your dashboard. A banner will remind you to complete your profile until you do.

To return to any onboarding step later:

1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Look for the "Complete your profile" section
3. Fill in any fields you skipped

<Callout type="tip">
Completing the workspace step (Step 2) unlocks the employee portal feature. If you plan to give employees their own login, it's worth doing this early.
</Callout>

## What happens after onboarding

Once you complete all three steps:

- Your dashboard is personalised with relevant shortcuts
- You can start generating documents immediately
- If you added your company country, Atlas Copilot will give jurisdiction-aware advice
- Your 14-day Pro trial is already running — make the most of it
