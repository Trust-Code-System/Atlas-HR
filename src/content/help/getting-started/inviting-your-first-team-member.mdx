Inviting a colleague to your Atlas HR workspace lets them access documents, run reports, and manage employee records alongside you. Here's how to do it.

## Before you invite

Team member invitations are a **Team plan feature**. If you're on Free or Pro, you'll need to upgrade first. Check **Settings → Billing** to see your current plan and upgrade options.

You also need to be a **workspace Admin** to send invitations. If you created the workspace, you're already an Admin.

## How to send an invitation

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Members** from the left sidebar.
2. Click **Invite member** in the top-right corner.
3. Enter the person's email address.
4. Choose their role (see below).
5. Click **Send invite**.
</Steps>

They'll receive an email from **hello@atlashr.app** with a link to accept the invitation.

## Choosing the right role

Atlas HR has two workspace roles:

**Admin**
- Full access to all workspace features
- Can invite and remove other members
- Can manage billing and workspace settings
- Can view, edit, and delete all employee records

**Member**
- Can access HR tools, the document library, and the Knowledge Hub
- Can view employee records they've been given access to
- Cannot invite others or change billing settings

<Callout type="tip">
For most small teams, give your co-HR manager Admin access and give other team members the Member role. You can change roles any time from Settings → Members.
</Callout>

## What happens when someone accepts

1. They click the invite link in their email.
2. If they don't have an Atlas HR account, they're prompted to create one (it takes 60 seconds).
3. They're added to your workspace automatically.
4. You'll see them listed under Settings → Members with the role you assigned.

## Invite link expiry

Invitation links expire after **7 days**. If your colleague didn't accept in time, go to Settings → Members, find their pending invite, and click **Resend**.

<Callout type="info">
Pending invitations are visible in Settings → Members under the "Pending" tab. You can cancel a pending invitation at any time if you need to change the email address or role.
</Callout>

## Removing a member

To remove someone from your workspace:

1. Go to **Settings → Members**.
2. Find the member and click the menu (three dots) next to their name.
3. Select **Remove from workspace**.

They'll immediately lose access to the workspace. Their own personal Atlas HR account (if they have one) is not affected.
