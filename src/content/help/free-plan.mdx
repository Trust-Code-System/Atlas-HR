## Free plan inclusions

The Atlas HR free plan gives you permanent access to core features at no cost:

| Feature | Free |
|---|---|
| Document generations | 3 per month |
| Copilot messages | 10 per day |
| Templates | Free templates only |
| Knowledge Hub | Full access |
| Community | Full access |
| Mini-HRIS | Not included |
| Team workspace | Not included |

Limits reset at the start of each calendar month (document generations) or each calendar day (Copilot messages).

## What triggers a limit notification?

When you reach your monthly generation limit, you'll see a prompt asking you to upgrade or wait until next month. Saved documents and previously generated content remain fully accessible.

## Upgrading from free

You can upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Upgrade mid-month and your generation count resets immediately. You won't be charged for the days you've already used on the free plan — you only pay from your upgrade date.

## Does the free plan expire?

No. The free plan is permanent. Your 14-day Pro trial automatically downgrades to free if you don't subscribe — it doesn't delete your account or data.

## Free plan vs trial

Your free trial gives you full Pro access for 14 days. The free plan is what you have after the trial ends (or if you signed up without starting a trial). They are different — the trial is temporary, the free plan is indefinite.
