## Save up to 33% with annual billing

Choosing annual billing pays for 12 months upfront at a reduced rate. The effective saving versus monthly billing:

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) | Saving |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pro | $15 | $10 | 33% |
| Team (base) | $49 | $39 | ~20% |
| Team (per seat) | $9 | $7 | ~22% |

Annual amounts are charged as a single payment at the start of each year.

## Switching from monthly to annual

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Switch to annual**.
3. Confirm the payment.

The charge is prorated: you pay the annual price minus a credit for the remaining days of your current monthly period. Access switches immediately.

## Switching from annual to monthly

You can schedule a switch to monthly billing that takes effect when your current annual period ends. To do this:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Switch to monthly**.

Your annual access continues until the anniversary date, then monthly billing begins.

## Annual plan renewal

Your annual plan auto-renews each year. You'll receive a renewal reminder email 30 days before the charge. To cancel before renewal, go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel plan**. Your access continues until the end of the paid year.

## Refunds on annual plans

If you cancel an annual plan, the remaining months are not automatically refunded. Refund requests within 14 days of the annual renewal date are considered case by case — [contact support](/help/contact).
