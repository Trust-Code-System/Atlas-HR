## Generation limits by plan

| Plan | Monthly limit |
|---|---|
| Free | 3 documents |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited |

The free plan limit resets at **midnight UTC on the 1st of each month**. Pro and Team plans have no monthly cap.

## What counts as one generation?

Each time you click **Generate** on any tool and the AI produces output, that counts as one generation — even if you discard the result. Regenerating with different inputs on the same tool counts as a second generation.

Saving a document to your library does **not** use an additional generation. Downloading a document you've already generated does **not** use a generation.

## Checking your usage

See your current month's generation count at **Settings → Usage** or in the sidebar on the Tools page.

## Approaching the limit

When you have 1 generation remaining on the free plan, a warning banner appears. When you reach 0, the Generate button is disabled and you see an upgrade prompt.

## Getting more generations

On the free plan, you can either:

1. **Wait** until the 1st of next month for the limit to reset.
2. **Upgrade** to Pro for unlimited generations — access is granted immediately after payment.

## Rate limiting on Pro and Team

Pro and Team plans don't have a monthly generation cap, but Atlas HR applies a rate limit to prevent abuse: no more than 60 generations per hour per account. This limit is invisible to normal users in typical usage.
