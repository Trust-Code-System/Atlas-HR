## Who can invite members?

Only **workspace admins** can send invitations. If you don't see an Invite button in Settings, ask your workspace admin to send the invite on your behalf or to promote you to admin.

## Sending an invitation

1. Go to **Settings → Members** in your workspace.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter the person's work email address.
4. Choose their role: **Admin** or **Member**.
5. Click **Send invite**.

The invitee receives an email with a secure link valid for 7 days. When they click the link, they'll be prompted to create an account (or sign in if they have one) and will be added to your workspace automatically.

## Roles explained

**Admin** — Can manage workspace settings, invite and remove members, manage billing (if the plan owner), and access all employee records in the Mini-HRIS.

**Member** — Can use tools, access templates, use Copilot, and view shared documents within the workspace. Cannot change settings or manage other members.

## Resending an invitation

If an invitee didn't receive the email or the link has expired:

1. Go to **Settings → Members**.
2. Find the pending invitation in the **Pending invites** section.
3. Click **Resend** next to their email.

A new 7-day link is generated. The old link is immediately invalidated.

## Revoking an invitation

To cancel a pending invitation before it's accepted, click **Revoke** next to the invite in **Settings → Members → Pending invites**.

## Removing an existing member

To remove an active workspace member, click the **···** menu next to their name and select **Remove from workspace**. Their account is not deleted — they simply lose access to your workspace.

## Plan seat limits

Team plan billing is per seat. Adding a new member mid-billing-cycle adds a prorated seat charge. See [Billing & plans](/help/articles/pro-plan-features) for seat pricing details.
