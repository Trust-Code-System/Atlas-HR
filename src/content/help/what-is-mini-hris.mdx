## Mini-HRIS in plain English

The Mini-HRIS (Human Resources Information System) is a lightweight employee management module built into the Atlas HR Team plan. It lets you store and manage employee records, track leave, and build an org chart — without a separate HRIS subscription.

## What's included

**Employee profiles** — Store each employee's personal details, role, department, start date, salary band, employment type, and emergency contacts.

**Org chart** — An automatically-generated org chart based on manager relationships in employee profiles.

**Leave management** — Track annual leave, sick leave, and other absence types. Employees can submit leave requests; managers approve or decline from within the platform.

**Documents** — Attach employment documents (contracts, offer letters, appraisals) to employee profiles.

**Lifecycle workflows** — Automate onboarding and offboarding checklists so nothing gets missed when someone joins or leaves.

**Approval workflows** — Route leave, expense, and document requests through a configurable approval chain.

## Who is it for?

The Mini-HRIS is designed for companies with 5–200 employees that:

- Don't need (or can't afford) an enterprise HRIS like Workday or BambooHR
- Currently track employees in spreadsheets and want a purpose-built system
- Already use Atlas HR for documents and Copilot, and want everything in one place

## What it's not

The Mini-HRIS is not a payroll system. It does not calculate or process payroll, submit RTI to HMRC, or connect to PAYE systems. If you need payroll, use a dedicated payroll provider (Xero Payroll, Gusto, Payfit, etc.) alongside Atlas HR.

## How to access it

Mini-HRIS is available to Team plan workspaces. Go to the **People** section in the main navigation once you have a Team plan workspace active.
