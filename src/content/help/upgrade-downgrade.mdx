## Upgrading your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade**. Select the plan and billing interval you want and complete the payment. Access upgrades immediately — no waiting for a billing cycle.

**Proration**: If you upgrade mid-cycle, you're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current period at the new plan price. Your next renewal date stays the same.

## Downgrading your plan

You can downgrade to a lower plan (e.g. from Team to Pro, or from Pro to Free) at any time.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription**.
2. Select **Change plan**.
3. Choose the lower plan.

Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing period** — you keep the higher-tier access until then.

## Adding seats (Team plan)

To add seats:

1. Go to **Settings → Members → Invite member**.
2. Add the new member's email.
3. A prorated seat charge is applied immediately for the remainder of the billing period.

Alternatively, go to **Settings → Billing → Manage seats** to pre-purchase additional seats.

## Removing seats

Seats are removed when you remove a member from the workspace. Removed seats are not automatically refunded mid-cycle but will not renew at the next billing date.

## What happens to data when downgrading

When moving from Team to Pro or Free:

- All saved documents and generated files remain intact.
- Employee records in the Mini-HRIS are preserved but inaccessible until you resubscribe to the Team plan.
- Workspace members lose access to the workspace (their individual accounts remain active).
- Scheduled reports are paused.
