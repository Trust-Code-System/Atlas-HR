Onboarding templates let you define a standard set of tasks for new joiners, then apply that template to any new employee so nothing slips through the cracks. Here's how to build one.

## What is an onboarding template?

An onboarding template is a reusable checklist of tasks. Each task has:

- A name (e.g. "Complete right to work check")
- A description with instructions
- An assigned role (HR, IT, Manager, or Employee)
- A due date rule (e.g. "Day 1," "Day 3," "Within 1 week of start date")
- A completion type (checkbox, document upload, or acknowledgement)

When you apply a template to a new employee, all tasks are created on their profile with calculated due dates based on their start date.

## Creating an onboarding template

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Lifecycle Templates**.
2. Click **New template**.
3. Select **Onboarding** as the template type.
4. Give the template a name (e.g. "UK Full-Time Onboarding" or "Remote Engineering Hire").
5. Click **Add task** and fill in the task details.
6. Repeat for each task.
7. Click **Save template**.
</Steps>

## Structuring your tasks

Think in three categories:

**Before Day 1 (pre-boarding):**
- Send welcome email
- Collect signed contract
- Set up IT equipment request
- Collect right to work documents

**Day 1:**
- Send login credentials
- Complete health and safety induction
- Introduce to the team
- Set up first week schedule

**First 30 days:**
- Complete probation review check-in at Day 30
- Acknowledge the employee handbook
- Complete mandatory training modules

<Callout type="tip">
Assign pre-boarding tasks to HR, Day 1 tasks split between HR and the employee's manager, and first-30-day tasks to the employee themselves. This spreads the load and gives everyone clear ownership.
</Callout>

## Assigning tasks to roles

For each task, choose the **assignee**:

- **HR** — visible to workspace Admins and Members
- **Employee's manager** — routes to whoever is set as the manager on the employee's profile
- **Employee** — appears on the employee's portal task list
- **IT** — use if you have an IT-specific workspace member

<Callout type="info">
Tasks assigned to "Employee" only appear if the employee has been sent a portal invite and has activated their account. If they haven't set up their portal, those tasks remain pending and won't be visible to them.
</Callout>

## Applying a template to a new employee

When you add a new employee (or at any point from their profile):

1. Open the employee's profile.
2. Click the **Tasks** tab.
3. Click **Apply template**.
4. Select the relevant onboarding template.
5. Confirm the start date — Atlas HR will calculate due dates automatically.
6. Click **Apply**.

All tasks appear on the employee's Tasks tab and (for employee-assigned tasks) on their portal.

## Having multiple templates

You can have as many templates as you need — for example:

- One template for UK office-based hires
- One for remote international contractors
- One for part-time retail staff

Choose the appropriate template when you apply it to each new joiner.
