Adding an employee to Atlas HR creates their record in the Mini-HRIS. This is the foundation for leave management, document tracking, onboarding tasks, and the employee portal.

## Before you begin

You need a **Team or Business plan** to access the People module. If you're on Free or Pro, go to Settings → Billing to upgrade.

You also need to be a workspace **Admin** or have People permissions as a Member.

## How to add an employee

<Steps>
1. Go to **People** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Add employee** in the top-right corner.
3. Fill in the required fields (see below).
4. Add any optional fields you want to capture.
5. Click **Save employee**.
</Steps>

The employee record is created immediately and appears in your People list.

## Required fields

These fields are needed to create the record:

- **Full name** — legal name as it should appear on documents
- **Job title** — their current role
- **Start date** — their employment start date (used for leave accrual calculations)

## Commonly used optional fields

You're not required to fill these in, but they make the record much more useful:

- **Email address** — needed if you want to send a portal invite
- **Department** — used for filters, reports, and org chart
- **Manager** — links to another employee record; used for approval workflows
- **Employment type** — full-time, part-time, contractor, etc.
- **Location / office** — useful for multi-site teams
- **Contract type** — permanent, fixed-term, probationary
- **Probation end date** — Atlas HR can flag this as a reminder

<Callout type="tip">
You don't need to fill in everything at once. Add the minimum (name, title, start date) to get the record created, and fill in the rest as you have the information. Incomplete fields are shown in an amber indicator on the profile.
</Callout>

## Sending an employee portal invite

Once the record is saved, you can invite the employee to set up their own portal login:

1. Open the employee's profile.
2. Click **Send portal invite** at the top of the profile.
3. They'll receive an email with a link to set their password and access their portal.

The portal gives employees the ability to submit leave requests, upload documents, view their record, and complete tasks assigned to them.

## Uploading documents to their record

You can attach documents to an employee profile right away:

1. Open the employee's profile.
2. Click the **Documents** tab.
3. Click **Upload document**.
4. Select the document type (contract, ID, right to work, etc.) and upload the file.

Documents you upload are visible to workspace Admins. If the employee has portal access, they can see documents you've marked as "Visible to employee."

## What about bulk import?

If you're adding more than a handful of employees at once, use the CSV import feature:

1. Go to **People → Import employees**.
2. Download the CSV template.
3. Fill it in with your employee data.
4. Upload the completed CSV and review the import preview.
5. Click **Import** to create all records.
