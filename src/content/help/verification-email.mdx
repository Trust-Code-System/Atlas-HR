## Check these things first

**Spam / junk folder** — Verification emails sometimes land in spam. Search for "Atlas HR" or "verify" in your junk folder.

**Correct email address** — Make sure you signed up with the email address you're checking. If you're not sure, try signing in at [atlashr.app/sign-in](/sign-in) to see what email you used.

**Wait a few minutes** — Email delivery can take up to 5 minutes during peak periods. Wait and then refresh your inbox.

## Resending the verification email

1. Go to [atlashr.app/sign-in](/sign-in).
2. Enter your email and try to sign in.
3. If your email is unverified, a banner appears with a **Resend verification email** button.
4. Click the button.

A fresh verification link is sent. The old link is invalidated.

## Email filters and corporate firewalls

Some corporate email systems block automated emails. Try:

- Checking your **All Mail** or **Promotions** tab in Gmail.
- Asking your IT team to whitelist `@atlashr.app` or `noreply@atlashr.app`.
- Signing up with a personal email address and linking your work email later in Settings.

## Verification link expired?

Verification links expire after **24 hours**. If yours has expired, request a new one using the **Resend verification email** button above.

## Still not receiving it?

[Contact our support team](/help/contact) with:

- The email address you signed up with
- Approximately when you signed up
- Confirmation that you've checked spam and tried resending

We can manually verify your email address after confirming your identity.
