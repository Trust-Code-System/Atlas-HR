Generation limits are probably the thing Free plan users ask us about most. Here's everything you need to know.

## How many generations do I get?

| Plan | Generations per month |
|---|---|
| Free | 5 |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited |
| Business | Unlimited |

The 5-generation limit on Free is a firm cap. Once you've used your 5 generations in a given month, the "Generate" button will show a prompt to upgrade instead of generating.

## What counts as a generation?

Every click of the **Generate** button in any Atlas HR tool counts as one generation. This includes:

- Job description generator
- Offer letter generator
- Disciplinary letter generator
- Performance improvement plan generator
- Contract generator
- Any other AI document tool

**Does not count:**
- Browsing templates in the template library
- Downloading a template without generating (no AI involved)
- Editing a document you already generated
- Using the **Regenerate section** button — this does count as a new generation

<Callout type="tip">
If you're on Free and want to make the most of your 5 monthly generations, spend a couple of minutes on the input form. A well-filled form means the output needs less editing, so you won't need to regenerate as much.
</Callout>

## When do limits reset?

Your generation count resets on the **same calendar date each month as your account creation date**. So if you created your account on the 14th, your generations reset on the 14th of each month.

You can see your current usage and reset date in **Settings → Account → Usage**.

## What happens if I run out mid-document?

If you've used your 5 generations and try to generate again, you'll see an upgrade prompt. You won't lose any work from previous generations — your already-generated documents are still there.

Your options at that point:

1. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited generations.
2. Wait until your limit resets next month.
3. Use a pre-made template from the template library (no generation needed — just download and edit manually).

## What about the 14-day Pro trial?

During your Pro trial, you get unlimited generations. When the trial ends and you move to Free (if you don't subscribe), your count resets to 0/5 for the new month.

<Callout type="info">
Pro trials start with unlimited generations from Day 1. Make the most of the trial period to generate all the documents your organisation needs — save them to your library so you have them even if you drop to Free.
</Callout>

## Checking your current usage

1. Go to **Settings → Account**.
2. Scroll to the **Usage** section.
3. You'll see generations used this month, remaining, and the reset date.
