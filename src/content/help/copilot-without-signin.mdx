## Sign-in is required for Copilot

Atlas Copilot requires a free Atlas HR account to use. You cannot access Copilot as a guest or anonymous visitor.

Creating an account is free and takes about two minutes — [sign up here](/sign-up). No credit card required.

## Why sign-in is required

Copilot requires an account because:

1. **Conversation history** — your conversations are saved so you can resume them later. This requires an authenticated identity.
2. **Fair-use enforcement** — free plan message limits (10/day) are per-account. Without authentication, limits cannot be enforced fairly.
3. **Data protection** — conversations may include sensitive employee data. Authentication ensures only you can access your conversation history.

## What you can do without signing in

Without an account you can still:

- Browse the [Knowledge Hub](/knowledge) (all articles are public)
- Browse [Templates](/templates) (view and preview free templates)
- Read [Help Center](/help) articles
- Read [Community](/community) posts (posting requires an account)

## Guest preview (beta)

We occasionally run a limited guest preview of Copilot for marketing purposes. If you see a "Try Copilot" feature on the homepage without signing in, this is a sandboxed demo with a small number of messages and no conversation history. Full Copilot always requires an account.

## Creating a free account

Go to [/sign-up](/sign-up), enter your email and a password, and verify your email address. Your free account gives you:

- 10 Copilot messages per day
- 3 document generations per month
- Full Knowledge Hub access

You can upgrade to Pro for unlimited access at any time.
