## Your conversations are private

Your Copilot conversations are private to you. No other Atlas HR users — including workspace admins and team members — can read your conversations.

## What data is stored?

Your conversation messages (both your inputs and Copilot's responses) are stored in our database so you can resume conversations later. Metadata such as timestamps and conversation titles are also stored.

## Does Atlas HR staff read my conversations?

Atlas HR staff do not routinely read your Copilot conversations. We may access conversation data in limited circumstances:

- When you contact support and share a conversation as part of a support request
- When investigating a reported abuse or safety concern
- When required by law

In all cases, access is logged and reviewed.

## Are conversations used for AI training?

Conversation data is **not** used to train any AI models by default. We do not sell conversation data to third parties.

If you participate in an explicit research or feedback programme (optional, always opt-in), anonymised data may be used with your consent.

## How long is data retained?

Conversation history is retained for 12 months from the date of the last message in the conversation. After that, inactive conversations are automatically deleted.

You can delete individual conversations at any time from the **Conversations** panel in Copilot.

## Requesting deletion of your data

To delete all your Copilot conversation history, go to **Settings → Data → Delete conversation history**. To delete your entire account and all associated data, see [How to delete your account](/help/articles/delete-account).

## Employee data you paste in

If you paste employee data (names, salaries, personal details) into Copilot, that data is transmitted to our AI provider to generate responses. Do not paste data that employees would not expect to be processed this way. Review our [Privacy Policy](/(legal)/privacy) for full details.
