## Start here

Go to [atlashr.app/sign-in](/sign-in) and try signing in again. Make sure you're using the correct email address — check for typos and confirm it matches the one you signed up with.

## Forgot your password?

1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (also check spam/junk).
4. Click the link and set a new password.

Password reset links expire after 1 hour. If yours has expired, request a new one.

## Email not verified yet?

If you recently signed up and haven't verified your email, your sign-in will fail. Look for the verification email in your inbox (subject: "Verify your Atlas HR account"). If you can't find it:

1. Go to [sign-in](/sign-in) and click **Resend verification email**.
2. Check spam/junk folder.
3. Allow up to 5 minutes for delivery.

## Signed up with Google?

If you created your account with **Continue with Google**, you must sign in the same way — entering your email and password won't work. Click **Continue with Google** on the sign-in page.

## Locked out after multiple failed attempts?

After several consecutive failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for security. Wait 15 minutes, then try again. If the problem persists, [contact support](/help/contact).

## Account doesn't exist?

If you see "No account found with this email", you may have signed up with a different email address. Try any other email addresses you use. If you're still stuck, [contact support](/help/contact) with your name and organisation.

## SSO / SAML login issues (Enterprise)

If your organisation uses SSO and you're having trouble, contact your IT administrator. They control the identity provider settings. If you believe the issue is on the Atlas HR side, have your admin [contact support](/help/contact).

## Still can't get in?

[Contact our support team](/help/contact) and include:

- The email address you're trying to sign in with
- What error message you see (a screenshot helps)
- Whether you signed up with email/password or Google
