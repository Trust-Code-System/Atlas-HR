Copilot message limits are straightforward, but here's exactly how they work so there are no surprises.

## How many messages do I get?

| Plan | Copilot messages |
|---|---|
| Free | 20 per day |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited |
| Business | Unlimited |

## What counts as a message?

Each time you send a message to Copilot — any message — that counts as one. Copilot's replies do not count against your limit, only your messages do.

So in a conversation, if you send 5 messages and Copilot replies 5 times, you've used 5 messages.

**Examples of what counts:**
- Asking a question
- Asking Copilot to revise or improve a draft it just produced
- Saying "thank you" or "can you expand on that?"

**Does not count:**
- Opening Copilot
- Reading a previous conversation
- Browsing conversation history

<Callout type="tip">
To get more out of your 20 daily messages, batch your questions. Instead of sending one question at a time, put 2–3 related questions in a single message. Copilot handles multi-part questions well.
</Callout>

## When does the daily limit reset?

The 20-message limit resets at **midnight UTC** every day. Your usage counter shows how many messages you've sent today and what time the reset happens in your local time zone.

You can see your current usage at the bottom of the Copilot interface, or under **Settings → Account → Usage**.

## What happens when I hit the limit?

When you've used your 20 messages for the day, Copilot shows a message explaining you've reached your daily limit and the time until it resets. The input field is disabled until the reset.

Your options at that point:

1. Wait until midnight UTC for the reset.
2. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited messages.
3. Use the Knowledge Hub to search for the answer manually.

## Does a partially answered message count?

If you send a message and Copilot starts responding but the page crashes or you navigate away before reading the response, that still counts as one message — Copilot generated the response even if you didn't see it.

## The 14-day trial

During your 14-day Pro trial, you have unlimited Copilot messages. When the trial ends and you drop to Free, the 20-per-day limit kicks in from that day.

<Callout type="info">
If you're on the Pro trial, the best use of unlimited Copilot access is to work through your backlog of HR questions, policy drafts, and document needs while you have no constraints. You can always revisit conversation history even after the trial ends.
</Callout>
