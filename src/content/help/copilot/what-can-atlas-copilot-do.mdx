Atlas Copilot is an AI assistant built specifically for HR professionals. It's trained on the Atlas HR knowledge base and understands HR terminology, common scenarios, and the kind of questions that come up in real HR work. Here's what it can help with.

## HR guidance and advice

Copilot can answer HR questions in plain language. Examples of what it handles well:

- "What's a reasonable notice period for a senior manager in the UK?"
- "Can I put someone on a PIP after their probation has ended?"
- "What are my obligations if an employee raises a grievance while on sick leave?"
- "How should I handle a situation where two employees have complained about each other?"

Copilot draws on Atlas HR's knowledge base and gives you a considered, practical answer — not just a generic definition.

<Callout type="info">
Copilot is context-aware within a conversation. If you ask a follow-up question, it remembers what you said earlier in the same session. Start a new conversation for a completely different topic.
</Callout>

## Document drafting

Copilot can write HR documents and letters directly in the chat. For example:

- "Draft a first written warning letter for repeated lateness."
- "Write an outcome letter for a disciplinary meeting."
- "Help me write an announcement for a team restructure."
- "Give me a template for a reference request email."

You can then copy the draft, refine it in the chat ("make it less formal" or "add a note about the appeal right"), and paste it into your document.

## Summarising knowledge articles

Ask Copilot to summarise a topic from the Knowledge Hub:

- "Summarise the article on redundancy process."
- "What does the 360-degree feedback guide say about rating calibration?"

Copilot retrieves relevant content from the Atlas HR knowledge base and gives you a digest — useful when you want the key points without reading a 3,000-word article.

## Policy drafting

Copilot can help you draft company policies:

- "Draft a flexible working policy for a 50-person UK company."
- "Write a section on disciplinary procedures for our employee handbook."
- "What clauses should our social media policy include?"

Use Copilot's draft as a starting point, then customise it for your organisation.

## What Copilot is not great at

- Precise legal advice (always verify with a qualified solicitor or employment lawyer)
- Real-time information (it doesn't browse the web; its knowledge has a training cutoff)
- Calculations (leave balance maths, payroll, or tax calculations)
- Tasks that require access to your employee records (Copilot doesn't have visibility into your HR data)

<Callout type="warning">
Copilot's advice is based on general HR practice and Atlas HR's knowledge base. It's a helpful starting point, but it is not a substitute for legal advice on complex or high-stakes situations. Always review important decisions with your employment legal team.
</Callout>

## Conversation history

Your Copilot conversation history is saved automatically. You can:

- Return to a previous conversation from the Copilot sidebar
- Search your history by keyword
- Delete individual conversations if you prefer not to keep them

Conversations are private to your account — they are not visible to your workspace members or the Atlas HR team.
