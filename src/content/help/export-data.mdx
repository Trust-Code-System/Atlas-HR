## Personal data export

To export your personal Atlas HR data:

1. Go to **Settings → Data**.
2. Click **Export my data**.
3. Confirm the export request.

You'll receive an email with a download link within 10 minutes. The link is valid for 48 hours.

## What's included in the export

The export is a ZIP file containing:

- `profile.json` — your account details (name, email, role, settings)
- `documents/` — all your generated and saved documents as PDF and raw text
- `conversations.json` — your Copilot conversation history
- `notifications.json` — your notification history

## Employee records export (Mini-HRIS)

Workspace admins can export the employee directory separately:

1. Go to **People → Employees**.
2. Click **Export**.
3. Choose **CSV** or **JSON**.
4. The file downloads immediately (no email).

The employee export includes all visible profile fields but excludes attached documents. To export documents attached to employee profiles, download them individually from each profile.

## Template and document library export

Your saved documents can be downloaded individually from **Library** (select a document and click Download). Bulk download is available on Team plan — select multiple documents and click **Download selected**.

## GDPR and data portability

Atlas HR supports the right to data portability under GDPR (Art. 20). The data export described above fulfils this right for personal data. If you need a more specific format or have questions about your GDPR rights, [contact support](/help/contact).

## After export

Your account and data remain active after an export. Exporting does not delete anything. If you want to delete your account after exporting, see [How to delete your account](/help/articles/delete-account).
