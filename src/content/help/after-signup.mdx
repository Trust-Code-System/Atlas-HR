## Your first few minutes

After you verify your email you'll land on your **dashboard** with an onboarding checklist. The checklist tracks your progress and surfaces the most useful features for your role.

Typical checklist items include:

- Complete your profile (name, role, organisation)
- Generate your first document using a tool
- Try Atlas Copilot with an HR question
- Invite a team member (Team plan)
- Connect your workspace settings

You can dismiss individual items once complete, or skip the checklist entirely via the **×** in the top-right corner.

## Your free Pro trial

All new accounts include a 14-day Pro trial. During the trial you can:

- Generate unlimited documents
- Use Atlas Copilot without daily message limits
- Access all premium templates
- Try the Mini-HRIS features (if you create a workspace)

No credit card is needed. At the end of the trial your account automatically drops to the **free tier** — your saved documents are preserved.

## Setting up your workspace

If you're part of an HR team, create a **workspace** from Settings → Workspace. A workspace lets you:

- Share a document library with your team
- Manage employee records in the Mini-HRIS
- Set organisation-level branding and defaults
- Assign admin and member roles

## What the free plan includes

Once your trial ends, the free plan gives you:

- 3 document generations per month
- 10 Copilot messages per day
- Access to free templates and knowledge articles
- Community access

Upgrade any time from [Settings → Billing](/workspace/settings/billing).
