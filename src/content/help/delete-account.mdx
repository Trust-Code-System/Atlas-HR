## Before you delete

Consider whether you need to delete your account or just want to stop paying. If cost is the concern, you can cancel your subscription and use the free plan indefinitely — your data stays intact.

If you want to take a break, simply stop using the platform. Your account and data are preserved at no cost on the free tier.

## Deleting your account

1. Go to **Settings → Account**.
2. Scroll to the **Danger zone** section.
3. Click **Delete account**.
4. Read the confirmation message.
5. Type your email address to confirm.
6. Click **Delete permanently**.

Your account is scheduled for immediate deletion. You'll receive a final confirmation email.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account:

- Your profile and all personal data
- All your generated documents and saved library items
- All your Copilot conversation history
- Your notification preferences and settings
- Any community posts and replies you made (anonymised or removed depending on content)

## What is not immediately deleted

- Billing records (retained for 7 years for legal/accounting purposes)
- Payment processor customer records (the processor's own retention policies apply)
- Anonymised analytics data that cannot be linked back to you

## Workspace accounts

If you're a workspace **owner**, you must either:

1. **Transfer workspace ownership** to another admin before deleting your account, or
2. **Delete the entire workspace** (which deletes all workspace members' access, employee records, and shared documents).

You'll be prompted to resolve this during the account deletion flow.

## Recovering a deleted account

Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. We cannot restore deleted accounts or their data. If you didn't mean to delete your account, [contact support immediately](/help/contact) — we may be able to halt deletion if it happened within the last few minutes.

## Data export before deletion

To keep a copy of your data before deleting, go to **Settings → Data → Export my data** and download your export. Allow up to 10 minutes for the export to be prepared.
