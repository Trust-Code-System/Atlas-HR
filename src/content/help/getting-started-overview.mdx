## What is Atlas HR?

Atlas HR is an AI-powered HR platform for HR professionals, people managers, and small business owners. It combines:

- **Document tools** — generate employment contracts, offer letters, performance reviews, disciplinary letters, and more in seconds using AI.
- **Atlas Copilot** — an HR-specific AI assistant that answers questions, helps you draft policies, and advises on employment law scenarios.
- **Knowledge Hub** — a library of expert HR guides covering labour law, HR best practices, and country-specific employment rules.
- **Mini-HRIS** — a lightweight employee record system for teams that need to manage headcount, leave, and org structure without a full HRIS.
- **Community** — a peer forum for HR practitioners to share questions, advice, and resources.

## Who is Atlas HR for?

- Solo HR managers handling everything themselves
- Small and medium-sized businesses that can't afford enterprise HRIS
- Founders and operators doing their own HR
- HR consultants managing multiple clients
- People teams that want AI to handle the drafting and compliance checking

## Your first week checklist

**Day 1 — Set up your account**
- Verify your email and complete your profile
- Generate your first document with an AI tool
- Ask Atlas Copilot your first HR question

**Day 2–3 — Explore the content**
- Browse the Knowledge Hub for articles relevant to your country or industry
- Bookmark templates you'll use regularly

**Day 4–5 — Set up your team (Team plan)**
- Create your workspace
- Invite your HR team members
- Import employee records into the Mini-HRIS

**Day 6–7 — Automate repetitive tasks**
- Set up scheduled reports for your leadership team
- Configure approval workflows for leave and expenses
