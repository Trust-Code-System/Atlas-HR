This article is written for **employees** using the Atlas HR portal to submit a leave request. If you're an HR admin looking to approve or manage requests, see [Managing leave requests](/help/articles/managing-leave-requests).

## Before you submit

Make sure you:

- Have access to your employee portal (if not, ask your HR team to send you an invite)
- Know the dates you want off
- Have discussed the request with your manager if your company requires this before submitting

## How to submit a leave request

<Steps>
1. Log in to your employee portal at the link your HR team sent you.
2. Click **Leave** in the top navigation.
3. Click **New leave request**.
4. Select the **leave type** — for example, Annual leave, Sick leave, or Compassionate leave.
5. Choose your **start date** and **end date** using the date picker.
6. If your request covers partial days, check the "Half day" option if available.
7. Add a **reason** (optional, but your employer may prefer you include one for certain leave types).
8. Click **Submit request**.
</Steps>

You'll see a confirmation message. Your request now appears in your Leave history as "Pending."

## Understanding the status on your request

Once submitted, your request moves through a review process:

- **Pending** — your request has been received and is waiting for the first approver to act
- **In review** — it's with your manager or HR team (this stage shows if there are multiple approvers)
- **Approved** — great news! Your leave is confirmed. You'll get an email notification.
- **Declined** — your request wasn't approved. You'll receive an email with the reason if one was given. You can submit a revised request if you want to try different dates.

<Callout type="info">
If you submitted a request and don't hear anything within a few days, follow up with your manager. Atlas HR sends automated reminders to approvers, but busy periods happen.
</Callout>

## Can I cancel a request I've already submitted?

Yes. While a request is still "Pending," you can cancel it:

1. Go to **Leave** in your portal.
2. Find the request.
3. Click **Cancel request**.

If the request has already been approved and you need to cancel it, contact your HR team or manager — you won't be able to cancel an approved request from the portal yourself.

## My leave balance looks wrong

Your leave balance is calculated based on your employment start date and your company's leave policy. If you think the balance is incorrect:

- Check if there's a carry-over from a previous year
- Remember that approved but not yet taken leave may still show in your balance
- Contact your HR team — only they can make manual balance adjustments

<Callout type="tip">
Your portal shows your balance by leave type (Annual leave, Sick leave, etc.). If a leave type is missing, it may not be configured for your role. Ask HR.
</Callout>
