The employee portal is a separate, simplified interface where employees can view their own HR information, submit leave requests, upload documents, and complete tasks assigned to them. Here's how to set it up for your team.

## Step 1: Make sure the employee has a record

Before you can invite an employee to their portal, they need to exist as an employee record in Atlas HR. If they're not added yet, see [How to add an employee](/help/articles/how-to-add-an-employee).

## Step 2: Send the portal invite

<Steps>
1. Go to **People** and open the employee's profile.
2. Click **Send portal invite** at the top of the profile.
3. Confirm the email address is correct — the invite goes to the email on the employee's record.
4. Click **Send**.
</Steps>

The employee receives an email from **hello@atlashr.app** with the subject "You've been invited to your HR portal."

<Callout type="tip">
If the employee's record doesn't have an email address yet, you'll be prompted to add one before sending the invite. Go to their profile → Edit → Email address.
</Callout>

## What the employee sees

The invitation email contains a secure link. When the employee clicks it:

1. They're taken to the Atlas HR portal setup page.
2. They create a password (or can use Google Sign-In if they prefer).
3. They're automatically logged in and their account is linked to their employee record — they don't need to search for or select their own profile.

The whole process takes under two minutes.

## What the employee portal shows them

Once logged in, the employee can see:

- **My profile** — their job title, department, start date, and any information you've marked as visible
- **Leave** — their leave balance, upcoming approved leave, and the option to submit new requests
- **Documents** — documents you've shared with them, and the option to upload documents you've requested
- **Tasks** — any onboarding or ongoing tasks assigned to them, with due dates
- **Handbook** — if you've shared a policy document for acknowledgement

They cannot see other employees' information or any of the HR admin interface.

## Invite link expiry

Portal invite links expire after **72 hours**. If the employee didn't click the link in time:

1. Go to their profile in Atlas HR.
2. Click **Resend invite**.

<Callout type="info">
Employees often miss the invite email in their junk folder, especially if their company email provider has aggressive spam filtering. Remind them to check spam or forward the email to personal email if needed.
</Callout>

## Revoking portal access

If an employee leaves and you want to immediately revoke their portal access:

1. Go to their profile.
2. Click the **...** menu (three dots) at the top.
3. Select **Revoke portal access**.

Their account is deactivated immediately. This is separate from archiving the employee record (which keeps the data but marks the employee as inactive).
