## Changing your email

1. Go to **Settings → Profile**.
2. Click **Edit** next to your current email address.
3. Enter your new email address.
4. Click **Save**.

You'll receive a verification email at the **new** address. Click the link in that email to confirm the change. Until you confirm, your old email address remains active.

## If you don't receive the verification email

- Check the spam/junk folder of the **new** email address.
- Make sure you typed the new email correctly — go back to Settings → Profile to check.
- Wait up to 5 minutes, then click **Resend verification email** on the Settings page.

## If you've lost access to your old email

If you can no longer access your current email account, [contact support](/help/contact) with proof of identity (e.g. your name, organisation, and billing details). We'll verify your identity and help you regain access.

## Email used for sign-in with Google

If you signed up using **Continue with Google**, your Atlas HR email is linked to your Google account. To change it, you would need to log in with a different Google account or set a password and unlink Google from **Settings → Security**. [Contact support](/help/contact) if you need help with this.

## What changes when you update your email?

- All future notifications go to the new address.
- Your login email becomes the new address.
- Invitations sent to the old address are not automatically transferred.

## Billing emails

Stripe invoices and billing notifications are sent to the email address on file at the time of the invoice. After changing your email, future billing emails go to the new address.
