## Adding employees one at a time

1. Go to **People → Employees** in the main navigation.
2. Click **Add employee**.
3. Fill in the required fields: **Full name**, **Email**, **Start date**, **Role/Job title**, and **Department**.
4. Click **Save**.

The employee profile is created immediately. You can add further details (salary band, emergency contacts, manager, documents) from the profile page at any time.

## Required vs optional fields

| Field | Required |
|---|---|
| Full name | Yes |
| Email | Yes |
| Start date | Yes |
| Job title | Yes |
| Department | Yes |
| Manager | No |
| Employment type | No |
| Salary band | No |
| National insurance / tax ID | No |

## Adding employees in bulk

If you're importing from a spreadsheet, use the **Import employees** option:

1. Go to **People → Employees**.
2. Click **Import**.
3. Download the CSV template.
4. Fill in the template with your employee data.
5. Upload the completed CSV.

Atlas HR validates the data before import and shows you a preview. Fix any errors shown and re-upload.

## Sending a portal invitation

After adding an employee, you can invite them to the employee portal (for leave requests and document access). Click **Invite to portal** on their profile. They'll receive an email invitation.

## Editing an employee profile

Click on any employee name to open their profile. Click **Edit** to modify their details. Changes are logged in the profile's audit history.

## Archiving vs deleting employees

When an employee leaves, **archive** rather than delete their profile. This preserves their history while removing them from active headcount counts.

To archive: open their profile and click **Archive employee**. Archived employees appear in the **Former employees** view.
