## 14 days of Pro, free

All new Atlas HR accounts start with a **14-day free trial of the Pro plan**. You get full access to every Pro feature from the moment you sign up — no credit card required.

## What's included in the trial

During your trial you can:

- Generate unlimited documents with any AI tool
- Use Atlas Copilot without daily message limits
- Access all premium templates
- Browse the full Knowledge Hub
- Participate in the community

If you're on a team trial you also get access to the Mini-HRIS, scheduled reports, and workspace features.

## When does the trial end?

Your trial runs for exactly 14 calendar days from the moment your account is created. You'll receive reminder emails at day 10 and day 13.

## What happens at the end of the trial?

If you haven't added a payment method and subscribed, your account automatically drops to the **free plan**:

- Generated documents remain in your library
- You keep access to free templates and Knowledge Hub
- Document generation is capped at 3 per month
- Copilot is limited to 10 messages per day

Nothing is deleted. Your data is safe.

## Subscribing during the trial

You can subscribe at any time during the trial from **Settings → Billing**. When you subscribe:

- Your trial ends immediately and billing begins
- Your 14-day anniversary is not charged twice — billing starts fresh from your subscribe date

## Starting a second trial

Free trials are one per account. If your trial has already ended, you can [contact support](/help/contact) if you believe there was an issue, but extensions are granted at our discretion.
