## Yes — cancel any time, no penalty

You can cancel your subscription at any time from **Settings → Billing**. There are no cancellation fees and no minimum commitment beyond your current billing period.

## How to cancel

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Manage subscription**.
3. Click **Cancel plan**.
4. You'll be asked to confirm and optionally share why you're cancelling.

Your subscription will be marked **cancels at period end**. You keep full access to your plan features until the current billing period ends.

## What happens after cancellation

- Your account moves to the **free plan** at the end of your paid period.
- All your saved documents, generated files, and profile data remain intact.
- Features above the free tier limits (unlimited generations, team workspace, Mini-HRIS) become inaccessible, but your data is preserved.
- If you resubscribe later, everything is exactly as you left it.

## Annual plan cancellations

If you're on an annual plan and cancel, your access continues until the end of the 12-month period you paid for. Annual plans are non-refundable for the remaining months unless you cancel within the first 14 days of a new annual period.

## Requesting a refund

For refund requests (e.g. accidental renewal, duplicate charges), [contact our support team](/help/contact) within 14 days of the charge. We handle refunds case by case.

## Reactivating after cancellation

To resubscribe after your plan has ended, go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade**. Your previous data, documents, and settings are restored immediately.
