## Sign up in two minutes

Go to [atlashr.app/sign-up](/sign-up) and enter your work email address and a password. You'll receive a verification email immediately — click the link inside to activate your account.

Once verified, you'll be walked through a short onboarding checklist that helps Atlas HR personalise your experience.

## Choosing a plan at signup

All new accounts start on a **14-day free trial of the Pro plan** — no credit card required. You can explore every Pro feature during your trial. After 14 days your account moves to the free tier unless you subscribe.

If you're signing up for a team, choose the **Team plan** during onboarding. You'll be prompted to create a workspace and invite your first colleagues.

## Google sign-in

You can also sign up using Google. Click **Continue with Google** on the sign-up page and authorise Atlas HR. Your Google email becomes your account email.

## After signing up

- Check your inbox for a welcome email with quick-start links.
- Complete the onboarding checklist in your dashboard to unlock all features.
- Visit [Settings → Profile](/workspace/settings) to add your name, role, and avatar.

## Troubleshooting signup

**Didn't receive the verification email?** Check your spam folder. If it's not there after five minutes, click **Resend verification email** on the sign-in page.

**Email already in use?** Your email may already have an account. Try signing in, or use [Forgot password](/forgot-password) to recover access.
