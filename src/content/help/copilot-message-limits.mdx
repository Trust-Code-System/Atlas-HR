## Message limits by plan

| Plan | Daily Copilot messages |
|---|---|
| Free | 10 per day |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Team | Unlimited |

Daily limits reset at **midnight UTC** every day.

## What counts as a message?

Each message you send to Copilot counts as one message. Copilot's replies do not count against your limit. In a typical back-and-forth conversation, you might use 5–8 messages to complete a task.

## What happens when you hit the limit?

Once you've used all 10 daily messages, the Copilot input box is disabled and shows a message explaining the limit. You can still read your previous conversation history.

Your limit resets automatically at midnight UTC — no action needed.

## Increasing your limit

Upgrade to the **Pro or Team plan** for unlimited Copilot messages. Go to **Settings → Billing** to upgrade. Access is immediate after payment.

## Conversation history

All your Copilot conversations are saved in the **Conversations** panel on the left side of the Copilot page. You can:

- Resume any previous conversation
- Delete conversations you no longer need
- Start a new conversation at any time

Conversations are private to you — they are not shared with your workspace or Atlas HR staff.
