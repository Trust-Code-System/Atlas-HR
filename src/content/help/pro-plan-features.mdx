## Pro plan at a glance

The Pro plan is designed for individual HR professionals who need unlimited access to tools and Copilot without managing a team workspace.

| Feature | Free | Pro | Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| Document generations | 3 / month | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Copilot messages | 10 / day | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Templates | Free only | All templates | All templates |
| Knowledge Hub | Full | Full | Full |
| Community | Full | Full | Full |
| Saved document library | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ (shared) |
| Mini-HRIS | — | — | ✓ |
| Team workspace | — | — | ✓ |
| Scheduled reports | — | — | ✓ |

## Pricing

Pro is billed per user:

- **Monthly**: $15/month
- **Annual**: $120/year (save 33%)

Annual plans are billed upfront. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from Settings → Billing.

## What "unlimited" means

Unlimited document generations means no monthly cap — you can generate as many documents as you need. Unlimited Copilot means no daily message limit. Atlas HR applies fair-use rate limits to prevent abuse; these are not visible to normal users in normal usage.

## Pro vs Team

Pro is a single-user plan. If you need to share a document library, manage employees in the Mini-HRIS, or give multiple colleagues access, you need the **Team plan**. Team pricing starts at a base fee plus a per-seat add-on.

## Upgrading to Pro

Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade to Pro**. Your card is charged immediately and access is granted within seconds.
