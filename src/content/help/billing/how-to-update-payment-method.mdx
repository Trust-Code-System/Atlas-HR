Updating your payment card or bank details takes about a minute through the Stripe billing portal. Atlas HR itself never stores your card information — everything is handled securely by Stripe.

## How to update your payment method

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Manage payment method** (or **Manage subscription**).
3. This opens the Stripe billing portal in a new tab.
4. Click **Add payment method**.
5. Enter your new card details and click **Add**.
6. If you want this to be the primary card, click **Set as default** next to the new card.
7. Optionally, remove the old card by clicking **Delete**.
</Steps>

Your next charge will use the new payment method automatically.

<Callout type="tip">
You can store multiple payment methods in the Stripe portal. This is handy if you want a backup card in case the primary one expires or is blocked.
</Callout>

## Accepted payment methods

Atlas HR currently accepts:

- Visa and Mastercard (debit or credit)
- American Express
- Other cards supported by Stripe in your region

Bank transfers and invoicing are available on the Business plan. Contact us if you need to pay by invoice.

## My card was declined — what do I do?

If your card is declined, you'll receive an email notification and a banner in your account. Here's what to check:

- **Insufficient funds** — make sure your card has enough credit or your current account has a sufficient balance.
- **3D Secure (3DS) required** — your bank may require you to approve the payment. Check your banking app for a pending authorisation.
- **Card expired** — update to a new card in the billing portal.
- **Blocked by your bank** — some banks flag international subscriptions. Contact your bank to allow charges from Stripe.

If your payment fails, Atlas HR gives a **7-day grace period** before downgrading your account. Update your payment method within this window and the charge will retry.

<Callout type="warning">
If your account is downgraded due to a failed payment, your employee records and documents are preserved. Resubscribing and paying will restore full access instantly.
</Callout>

## Receipts and invoices

All payment receipts are emailed to your account email automatically. You can also download past invoices from the Stripe billing portal under "Billing history."
