Here's an honest summary of our refund policy. We try to be fair.

## Annual plans — 14-day refund window

If you subscribed to an **annual plan** and decide it's not right for you within the first **14 days**, you can request a full refund. No awkward questions, no partial refund calculation.

**To request a refund:**
1. Go to **Settings → Billing** and cancel your subscription (so you don't get charged again).
2. [Contact support](/help/contact) with your account email and the subject line "Refund request."
3. We'll process the refund within 5–10 business days. It goes back to the original payment method.

After 14 days on an annual plan, refunds are not available — but you'll keep access until the end of the annual period.

<Callout type="info">
The 14-day window starts from the date your subscription was charged, not from when you first signed up. If you were on a free trial before subscribing, the clock starts when the first payment is taken.
</Callout>

## Monthly plans — no refunds

Monthly subscriptions are not eligible for refunds. Because monthly billing gives you flexibility to cancel at any time without a long-term commitment, we don't offer mid-cycle refunds.

If you cancel a monthly subscription, you keep access until the end of the period you already paid for.

## Free trial — no charge, nothing to refund

If you're still on the free 14-day Pro trial and didn't enter payment details, there's nothing to refund. Your account simply moves to Free at the end of the trial.

If you did enter payment details during the trial and were charged before you expected, contact us — that's something we'll look into right away.

## Billing errors

If you believe you were charged incorrectly — for example, you were billed for a plan you didn't choose, or charged twice — we want to know immediately. Contact us with your account email and the charge details, and we'll investigate within one business day.

<Callout type="warning">
Don't raise a chargeback with your card issuer before contacting us. Chargebacks create complications for both sides. We resolve billing errors quickly and fairly — just reach out first.
</Callout>

## Exceptions

We handle genuine hardship situations case by case. If you're going through an unexpected circumstance — redundancy, business closure, medical emergency — reach out and explain the situation. We'll do our best to find a reasonable solution.
