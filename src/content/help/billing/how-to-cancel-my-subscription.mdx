You can cancel your Atlas HR subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees, and you keep access until the end of your current billing period.

## How to cancel

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Under your current plan, click **Manage subscription**.
3. This opens the secure billing portal.
4. Click **Cancel plan**.
5. Choose whether to cancel immediately or at the end of your billing period (we recommend end of period so you keep what you've paid for).
6. Confirm the cancellation.
</Steps>

You'll receive a cancellation confirmation email. Your subscription status in Settings → Billing will update to show the date your access ends.

## What happens when you cancel

**Immediately:**
- Nothing changes. You keep full access until your billing period ends.

**At the end of your billing period:**
- Your account moves to the Free plan automatically.
- Document generation resets to 5 per month.
- Copilot messages reset to 20 per day.
- Employee records, leave history, and documents are **not deleted** — they're preserved so you can access them if you resubscribe.
- Team members lose workspace access (they can still use their own personal accounts).

<Callout type="info">
Your data is retained for 12 months after cancellation on a paid plan. If you resubscribe within that window, everything is exactly as you left it.
</Callout>

## What about the employee portal?

If you're on Team or Business and employees have portal access, their logins will stop working once your subscription ends. It's a good idea to notify your employees before cancelling.

## How to resubscribe

Changed your mind? Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade**. Select your plan, enter payment details, and you're back. If you're within the 12-month retention window, your employee records and history will all be there.

<Callout type="tip">
If you're cancelling because of cost, contact us first. We occasionally have options that aren't listed publicly — particularly for teams going through budget reviews.
</Callout>

## Cancelling a free trial

If you're on the 14-day Pro trial and don't want to be charged, you don't need to do anything — the trial ends automatically and your account moves to Free with no charge. But if you entered payment details and want to make sure you're not billed, follow the same cancellation steps above.
