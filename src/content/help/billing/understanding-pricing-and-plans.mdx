Atlas HR pricing is designed to be transparent. This article breaks down exactly what you get on each plan and where the limits sit.

## Free plan

Free is for individuals who want to explore what Atlas HR can do without committing.

**What's included:**
- 5 AI document generations per month
- 20 Atlas Copilot messages per day
- Full access to the Knowledge Hub and templates (view only)
- 1 user seat
- Community access

**What's not included:**
- Document library (saving and organising generated documents)
- Employee records (Mini-HRIS)
- Team members
- Approval workflows
- Reports

The Free plan has no time limit — it's free forever, not a time-limited trial.

## Pro plan

Pro is for HR professionals who use Atlas HR regularly and need unlimited generations.

**What's included:**
- Unlimited AI document generations
- Unlimited Atlas Copilot messages
- Document library with search and folders
- Template customisation and saving
- CSV export
- 1 user seat
- Priority response in community forums

**Limits:**
- Still 1 seat — Pro is a personal plan
- No employee records or Mini-HRIS
- No approval workflows

## Team plan

Team adds a full lightweight HRIS on top of Pro.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Pro
- Up to **10 admin/member seats**
- Mini-HRIS (employee profiles, leave management, document storage, compliance tracking)
- Employee portal (employees get their own login)
- Onboarding templates and task tracking
- Approval workflows for leave and documents
- Reports: headcount, leave, turnover, compliance

**Limits:**
- Maximum 10 workspace seats
- Single workspace (one organisation)

## Business plan

Business is for HR consultancies, agencies, and larger HR departments.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Team
- Unlimited seats
- Multiple workspaces (manage different clients or entities separately)
- Custom branding on generated documents
- API access for integrations
- Priority support channel with dedicated response time

## Billing cycles

- **Monthly** — billed on the same date each month, cancel any time
- **Annual** — billed once per year at a discounted rate, saves approximately two months compared to monthly

You can switch from monthly to annual at any time from Settings → Billing. The switch takes effect at your next billing cycle.

## What counts toward the generation limit?

Every time you click "Generate" in any Atlas HR tool — whether for a job description, offer letter, disciplinary letter, or any other document — that counts as one generation. Re-generating the same document after editing inputs counts as a new generation.

Browsing templates without generating does **not** count.

On Free, generations reset on the same calendar date each month (your account creation date). On Pro and above, there is no reset because there's no limit.

<Callout type="tip">
If you're approaching your free limit and have a time-sensitive document to create, consider generating it slightly differently (different inputs) to refine the output. All 5 generations give you a full document — there's no such thing as a "half generation."
</Callout>
